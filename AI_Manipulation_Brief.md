## II. Social Media Algorithmic Feed Manipulation

### Primary Evidence Source
Cross-platform research verified through engineering documentation and leaked files

### Documented Capabilities (Confirmed as operational, November 2025)
- **Real-time feed optimization** in milliseconds (200ms-1.5s response time)
- **Massive candidate processing**: 10,000-100,000 posts evaluated to select what appears in feed
- **Behavioral micro-profiling**: Touch tracking, dwell time monitoring, typing speed analysis
- **Predictive engagement models**: High-accuracy prediction of content that keeps users scrolling
- **Outrage amplification**: Algorithms explicitly optimized for engagement in ways that amplify outrage (confirmed via Facebook Papers)
- **AI-generated content injection**: Meta actively deploying AI-generated profiles and content into feeds

### Documented Weaknesses
- **Overconfident speculation presented as fact**: Initial research mixed confirmed capabilities with unverified claims (Grok claimed heart rate monitoring, generative content injection, and individual A/B testing as "Already Live" - only basic micro-profiling confirmed at scale)
- **Hallucination in technical claims**: AI systems confidently stated features as deployed when evidence only showed technical feasibility
- **Verification gap**: Distinction between "technically possible," "confirmed in testing," and "deployed at scale" frequently blurred

### Fact-Check Results
- Micro-profiling basics (dwell time, clicks, typing): **Confirmed**
- Heart rate monitoring via sensors: **Technically possible, not confirmed in social apps**
- 50+ parallel A/B tests on individual users: **Not documented at that scale**
- AI-generated content injection that gets deleted: **No evidence**

### Implications
Even with overcorrection for hallucinations, confirmed capabilities demonstrate:
- Platforms process thousands of content options in real-time to manipulate what users see
- Behavioral tracking granular enough to predict and exploit psychological vulnerabilities
- Optimization explicitly designed to maximize engagement, not accuracy or user wellbeing
- Infrastructure exists for sophisticated manipulation; debate is only over scope of deployment
